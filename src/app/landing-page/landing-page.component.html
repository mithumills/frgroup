About F&R Logistics


With 10+ years of experience spread across the interstate carrier, same-day local courier, and freight management industries, our experience equips us to help our clients navigate the stresses and complexities of the supply chain.

The recent past has emphasised the need to be flexible in our service offering and reliable in our attitude and commitment.

  • Flexibility with carrier options, carrier services, and a freight partner who is willing to think outside the square to get your product delivered!
  • Reliability does not just relate to carriers; it's critical to who you entrust to partner with to provide carrier options. Some things in the supply chain can not be controlled-our dedication in our approach to our customers can be!
